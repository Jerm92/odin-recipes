Coconut Flour Cookies

The best grain-free cookies you'll ever have

If your're trying to watch what you eat but still crave a delicous cookie then this grain-free recipe is a must try.

Coconut flour is a gluten-free flour made solely from coconuts. It's high in fiber and is a good source of protein. Adding it to your diet may promote stable blood sugar, good digestion, and heart health. If you've never thought of cookies as a health food, maybe after this you will

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup coconut flour
  • 1/3 cup brown coconut sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted & cooled
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350ÂºF and grease a cookie sheet.
  2. In a bowl, combine coconut flour, brown sugar, baking soda and salt.
  3. Add in eggs, butter, vanilla and chopped chocolate. Combine until a thin dough forms.
  4. Wet hands and roll dough into 9 balls, if you just spoon the cookies onto the baking sheet without rolling them into balls, the cookies won't come out pretty and smooth. Be sure to roll them into balls.
  5. Bake for 12-14 minutes, or until the bottom of the cookies turn golden brown.
